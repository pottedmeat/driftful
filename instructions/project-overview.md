*   **Description:** I’m building a mobile-first web app for quickly tracking tasks, notes, and events for minimalist scatterbrains and Bullet Journal enthusiasts that helps users quickly enter and manage date-related items as well as rediscover misplaced intentions.
*   **Primary Goal:** Driftful is designed to offer a minimal, rapid, and intuitive experience for Bullet Journal enthusiasts, enabling fast entry, effortless migration of tasks, and clear organization by day, week, month, year, or collections without the overhead of feature-heavy managers.
